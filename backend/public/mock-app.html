📋 Job Description
Senior AI Engineer – Acme Corp (New York, NY / Remote)

Acme Corp is looking for a Senior AI Engineer to join our applied AI team. You will design, build, and deploy production-grade AI systems that power our core product offerings. You will collaborate closely with product, data science, and engineering teams to bring research concepts to reliable, scalable production deployments.

Responsibilities:

  • Design and implement ML pipelines and LLM-based features in Python and JavaScript.
  • Build RESTful APIs and backend services to serve AI models at scale.
  • Own reliability, monitoring, and observability of deployed AI systems.

Requirements:

  • 3+ years of software engineering experience (Python, Node.js, or equivalent).
  • Experience with LLMs (OpenAI, Gemini, Claude) and RAG architectures.

Compensation: $140,000–$180,000 / year + equity. Remote-friendly.
